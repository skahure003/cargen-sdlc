---
title: Roles And Accountability
description: Responsibilities shared by requesters, approvers, implementers, and auditors.
weight: 2
---

Delivery controls work only when ownership is explicit.

- Requesters define scope, business justification, and rollback planning.
- Approvers verify readiness and acceptable residual risk.
- Implementers execute the plan and capture evidence.
- Auditors confirm records remain complete and reviewable.
